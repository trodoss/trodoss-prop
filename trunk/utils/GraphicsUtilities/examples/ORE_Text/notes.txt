The ORE [Oldbitcollector Retro (Game) Engine] Text driver is not "feature complete," and has not been fully tested/released.
The developent of this engine is in the "MSPacman" thread on the Hydra forums.

To generate the font from the simple_2bpp_font.bmp bitmap Enter:
FontGenerator simple_2bpp_font.bmp Font_Pacman.spin O
